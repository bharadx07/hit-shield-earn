Hit Shield Earn

An action packed battle game.

Rules

1 player mode
In this mode you are playing against the computer.
-The game starts with each player having 100 lives and $850.

-On your turn, you can do one of four things: Use your money to attack your opponent with a selection of wepons (With each one costing different amounts of money), Use your money to either buy lives or shield from anything for 1 turn, and finally buy income boosters (you get income after both peoples turn: $100).

-Once you make your move the computer will make its move. The computer will make a move determined by pre-programmed logic. It is not coded to be super easy or super hard to beat.

-These actions will continue until one player (computer or player) hits zero or lower lives.

2 player mode

-Same as one player mode but instead of playing against a pre-programmed bot, you play against another player.

-Enjoy the game. If any bugs are recognized, you can email me at sduggarajub@gmail.com!
